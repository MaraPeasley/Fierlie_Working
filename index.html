We are a thoughtful collection of edible and practical goods sourced from local growers, creators, and artists.

Enthusiasts are introduced to an array of transcendent flavor experiences through guided tastings and collaborations. Bringing forth discoveries to the kitchen table of local olive oils, botanically infused syrups, small batch spoon sweets, and wildcrafted teas. Each product will be specially selected and available for purchase individually, or in whimsical gift arrangements.

Our Featured Products:

Wild Coast Brew

Southern Oregon wildcrafted and cultivated tea blends.

The unique teas at Wild Coast Brew are created with those primordial stories in mind. Each artisan small-batch tea blend celebrates an archetype, passed down through story and shared imagination, with an offering of befitting herbs, fungi, and spices. Alchemy in a cup.

Meadowland Syrups

Central Oregon infused botanical syrups for cocktails and coffee.

Meadowland is a collaboration between Katie and Kathy, based out of Bend, Oregon. Both work together to develop unique and delicious flavors that are deeply inspired by Katie's rural upbringing on the prairie. They hope their simple syrups conjure feelings of joy, wonder, and warm memories of summers past.

Durant Olive Mill

Yamhill County grown and milled, single varietal and fused, extra virgin olive oils.

For 50 years, our family has grown wine grapes, acting as stewards of the land on this hill we call home. Since then, our vision has grown to include Durant Vineyards Winery & Tasting Room, award-winning Durant Olive Mill extra virgin olive oil, Durant Kitchen, Durant Body Care, Red Ridge Farms Specialty Plant Nursery, Farm Shop, overnight lodging, expansive gardens, and the Durant Nature Trail.

Romancing the Jam

Benton County grown fruit and berries in small batches.

A jam supplier and manufacturer for many local businesses in Beaverton and beyond. I provide all sorts of raw ingredients to my clients in their efforts to create the best possible jams for you, whether that’s freshly cooked hot conserves or things for making jam in your own home.

Bird Mafia

Portland artist creates papercut design prints on flour sack towels, vinyl stickers and canvas totes.

BIRD MAFIA is a Portland, Oregon based design company inspired by the intricate and unique papercutting art form. Every Bird Mafia design begins as hand-cut paper before making it's way into our collection. Founded in 2009 amid the handmade revolution in indie craft fairs and the DIY movement, operations began small. Today the Bird Mafia line can be found in stores throughout Portland and the United States.

Moonbeam Kitchen

Handmade in Albany in lovely fabrics as microwave cozies, casserole and loaf totes.
