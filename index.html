Fierlie

“genius loci” or “spirit of the place. Conceived in a climate of possibility, born from rain & sun, rooted in the soil of our home region.


We are a thoughtful collection of edible and practical goods sourced from local growers, creators, and artists. Enthusiasts are introduced to an array of transcendent flavor experiences through guided tastings and collaborations. Bringing forth discoveries to the kitchen table of local olive oils, botanically infused syrups, small batch spoon sweets, and wildcrafted teas. Each product will be specially selected and available for purchase individually, or in whimsical gift arrangements.
